Lucille Huang


Lucille Huang is a PhD Candidate at the Graduate Institute of Life Sciences, National Defense Medical University (NDMU), Taiwan. Under the supervision of Prof. Chih-Chin Shih, her doctoral research investigates the molecular mechanisms by which Saccharina japonica-derived fucoidan (SJF) modulates early platelet activation to promote burn wound healing.

Her work bridges hemostasis, coagulation biology, and regenerative medicine, combining in vitro platelet function assays with in vivo burn animal models to elucidate novel therapeutic pathways.
