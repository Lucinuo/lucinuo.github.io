## About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Graduate Institute of Life Sciences, National Defense Medical University (Taiwan), supervised by **Prof. Chih-Chin Shih**. My research focuses on the molecular mechanisms by which *Saccharina japonica*-derived fucoidan (SJF) modulates early platelet activation to enhance burn wound healing.

My work bridges marine bioactives, platelet biology, and translational wound care research.
